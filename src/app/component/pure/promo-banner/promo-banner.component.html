Hello, it's me amango134. I created this site some time ago because I wanted to use my iPad and Kiiroo Keon together.

Now, after lots of time in development, I'm happy to introduce a native Mac script player called FunPlayer. If you are an Apple user, I trust you won't regret checking this out.

It syncs your videos with funscripts and supports The Handy over Wi-Fi and many Bluetooth devices via Intiface.
